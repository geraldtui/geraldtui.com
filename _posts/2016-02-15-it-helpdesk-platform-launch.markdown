Led the project to launch the organization's first IT Helpdesk platform (Freshservice),
owning the rollout from stakeholder buy-in through to adoption. Introducing structured
ticketing and support workflows where none existed before, the project transformed how
the business raised, tracked, and resolved technical support.

Highlights:

- Delivered the rollout on schedule, centralizing 100% of support requests within 60 days.
- Drove adoption and defined the ticketing, SLA, and reporting processes for IT support.
- Coordinated stakeholders across the business to embed the new support workflows.
- Improved visibility and responsiveness across a diverse infrastructure stack.
