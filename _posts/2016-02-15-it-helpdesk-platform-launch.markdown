Led the launch of the organization's first IT Helpdesk platform (Freshservice),
introducing structured ticketing and support workflows where none existed before. The
rollout transformed how the business raised and tracked technical support.

Highlights:

- Centralized 100% of support requests within 60 days.
- Introduced structured ticketing, SLAs, and reporting for IT support.
- Improved visibility and responsiveness across a diverse infrastructure stack.
