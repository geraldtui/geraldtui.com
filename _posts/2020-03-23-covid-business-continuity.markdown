When the COVID-19 pandemic hit, I implemented the organization's critical business
continuity plan within 24 hours, enabling the entire workforce to keep operating remotely
without interruption. It was a high-pressure test of leadership, coordination, and
communication across the whole business.

Highlights:

- Transitioned 200+ employees to full remote operations within 24 hours.
- Kept a global ERP software business fully operational through disruption.
- Coordinated and communicated effectively across the entire organization.
