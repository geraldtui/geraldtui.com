Established Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) as a core tenet of platform deployment using
Terraform and Terragrunt. Codifying the platform end-to-end made deployments fast,
repeatable, and auditable, and removed slow manual configuration steps.

Highlights:

- Cut infrastructure deployment time from hours to minutes.
- Increased engineering velocity through repeatable, version-controlled infrastructure.
- Standardized environments with reusable Terraform/Terragrunt modules.
