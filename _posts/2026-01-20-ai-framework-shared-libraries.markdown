Developed an AI framework for the Kubernetes platform teams to guide how AI is adopted
and used day to day, paired with a centralised, shared set of tools and libraries. The
framework gave the platform teams a common foundation and clear best practices, turning
ad-hoc experimentation into a repeatable, governed capability.

Highlights:

- Defined an AI usage framework with guardrails, patterns, and best practices for the Kubernetes platform teams.
- Built a centralised library of shared tools to standardise AI workflows and reduce duplicated effort.
- Standardised on Cursor and Claude to accelerate adoption and keep developer experience consistent.
- Enabled the platform teams to move faster with AI while maintaining quality and responsible-use standards.
