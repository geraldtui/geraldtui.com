Directed the complete migration of the business from on-premises Microsoft Exchange to
Microsoft 365, moving email, collaboration, and identity into the cloud for a global ERP
software vendor with offices across multiple regions. The project retired ageing
on-premises infrastructure in favour of a scalable, cloud-first foundation.

Highlights:

- Migrated 200+ users from on-premises Exchange to Microsoft 365 with minimal disruption.
- Decommissioned on-premises mail infrastructure, cutting maintenance and hardware overhead.
- Established a scalable, cloud-first foundation for collaboration and identity.
- Improved remote accessibility and resilience for a globally distributed workforce.
