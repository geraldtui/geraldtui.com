Directed the complete migration of the business from on-premises Microsoft Exchange to
Microsoft 365, moving email, collaboration, and identity into the cloud for a global
ERP software vendor with offices across multiple regions.

Highlights:

- Migrated 200+ users from on-premises Exchange to Microsoft 365 with minimal disruption.
- Modernized collaboration and identity management on a cloud-first foundation.
- Reduced on-premises maintenance burden and improved remote accessibility.
