Developed and successfully delivered a comprehensive five-year IT Business Policy that set
the strategic direction for the organization's technology. The policy gave stakeholders a
clear, long-term roadmap and governance framework.

Highlights:

- Defined strategy across performance, security, disaster recovery, usage, and scalability.
- Aligned IT initiatives with the priorities of business stakeholders.
- Established governance to guide investment and risk decisions for five years.
