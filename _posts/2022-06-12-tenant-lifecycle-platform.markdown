Designed, developed, and maintained a critical, end-to-end software platform that powered
tenant lifecycle management for Workday customers. As a full-stack engineer I built the
services and interfaces that managed provisioning and configuration at scale, focused on
reliability and a clean operational experience for the teams and customers who depend on it.

Highlights:

- Built an end-to-end full-stack platform on Node.js running on Kubernetes.
- Delivered tenant provisioning and configuration capabilities at enterprise scale.
- Engineered for service reliability across customer-facing environments.
- Shipped features that replaced fragile, manual operational steps with robust software.
