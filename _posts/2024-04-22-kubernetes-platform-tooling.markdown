Automated the operational workflows on top of Workday's largest Kubernetes platform,
turning tasks that previously required platform-team intervention into self-service
actions. The automation gave engineering teams the capabilities they needed without deep
infrastructure knowledge, cutting manual toil and accelerating delivery.

Highlights:

- Automated common platform operations into self-service workflows for thousands of internal services.
- Reduced manual platform support requests by removing repetitive, error-prone steps.
- Packaged repeatable, automated deployments with Helm for consistency.
- Freed the platform team from routine toil to focus on higher-value engineering.
